About Our Practice

At Valenzuela Chiropractic Center, we are more than just a chiropractic clinic - we are your partners in health and wellness.

Who We Are:

We are a team of experienced and compassionate chiropractors who are passionate about improving the health and well-being of our community. With our personalized approach to care, we aim to address the root cause of discomfort and empower our patients to live healthier, pain-free lives.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to promote health, wellness, and vitality in our community through chiropractic care. We are committed to delivering personalized treatments that focus on the individual needs of each patient, helping them achieve their health goals and live life to the fullest.

Our Values:

  1. Compassion: We genuinely care about the well-being of our patients and are dedicated to providing them with the highest level of care and support.
  2. Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in everything we do, ensuring that our patients always receive honest and transparent care.
  3. Excellence: We are committed to excellence in all aspects of our practice, from the quality of our treatments to the level of customer service we provide.

Our History

Serving the Valenzuela since 1990, we have helped thousands of patients, relieving pain and improving their overall health. Our Doctors of Chiropractic practiceprimarily Diversified, Gonstad and Thompson chiropractic techniques. We believe in full body spine techniques. Our primary concerns over the years have been improving your health and maintaining our reputation. We care about you as an individual, providing specialized care for your needs at an affordable price. We are transparent and trustworthy. All fees are up front, NO HIDDEN FEES. With the guidance of VCC well-trained and experienced doctors, you are in control of your health care.
